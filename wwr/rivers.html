Chattahoochee

The Chattahoochee is a local legend for white water rafting. We float a dam-released section of the river, which brings a calm river in the morning, and wild rapids later in the day. Whether you're new or experienced, the Chattahoochee is a great option!

Chattooga

The Chattooga is gorgeous. Being the filming location for the movie "Deliverance", this trip has a variety of different rapid levels, spanning from 1 to 3, providing a good time for any adventurer.

Toccoa

The Toccoa is our calmest trip. It's meant for the faint of heart and the family-friendly. This Blue Ridge river offers families a chance to get away from every-day life and peacefully float the river with their loved ones.
